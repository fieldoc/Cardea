Biometric Wave HeroThe ECG waveform is a living element — it pulses with a subtle animation that makes the screen feel alive. Session data floats on top of the heartbeat. The gradient follows the exact Cardea 4-stop spectrum across the wave.
Pulse ThreadA thin gradient line connects the ECG hero to the stat chips below, reinforcing the biometric theme throughout. The stat chips sit ON the heartbeat.
State: Active Bootcamp, Today's SessionThis is the most energizing state — the waveform is bright and active, the CTA is prominent. On rest days, the ECG would flatten to a calm sine wave with muted colors.
Accountability Partner SlotPartner's recent activity could appear as a ghost pulse line behind yours — their heartbeat overlaid on your ECG. Subtle but emotionally resonant.
Radial Mission ControlThe progress ring becomes THE hero — 3x the current size. Stats orbit around it as satellite dots. Ring color shifts per phase: blue for BUILD, green for TAPER, violet for BASE.
Rest Day TreatmentNo "Start Session" button on rest days — the next session appears as a muted ribbon below the ring. This encourages recovery without guilt.
State: Week 8, BUILD PhaseThe blue-to-cyan gradient reinforces the BUILD phase identity. At 67%, the ring is visually satisfying but clearly shows more journey ahead.
Accountability Partner SlotPartner's progress ring could overlap as a thin outer arc — you'd see both completion percentages at a glance. Their satellite dot shows their streak count.
Bold Editorial TypographyGiant serif type creates emotional impact for the empty state. The gradient word ("MILE") is the eye-catcher. This feels like opening a luxury fitness magazine, not a dashboard.
Clean HierarchyOne primary CTA (gradient fill), one ghost secondary. No clutter. The empty stats section is honest, not hidden — it creates anticipation to fill them in.
State: No Active BootcampThis is what new users and graduated users see. The typography makes an empty screen feel intentional and premium, not broken.
Accountability Partner Slot"Run with a friend" as a third CTA option. Or "Join [Name]'s bootcamp" if invited. Editorial style makes social CTAs feel premium, not pushy.
Elevation Journey MapProgress visualized as a mountain climb. The ascending path shows where you've been (solid gradient line) and where you're going (dashed green). Your position dot pulses at Week 10.
Taper Phase EnergyGreen palette calms pre-race anxiety. Reduced volume targets shown explicitly. The coaching tone shifts from "push harder" to "trust the taper" — emotionally aware.
State: Week 10, Near FinishThe summit flag is VISIBLE — you can almost reach it. The dashed remaining path is short. This creates genuine emotional payoff for users who've trained for 10 weeks.
Accountability Partner SlotPartner shown as a second dot on the mountain path. You can see if they're ahead or behind. Creates gentle competition without pressure.
