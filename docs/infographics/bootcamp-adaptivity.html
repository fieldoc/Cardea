How Cardea Adapts Your Training

Every bootcamp session is shaped by your fitness, your phase, and your tier. Here's how the engine decides what you run.

TSB = Training Stress Balance (CTL − ATL). Negative = fatigued, positive = fresh.
EF trend = Efficiency Factor slope over last 42 days. Rising EF = getting fitter at the same effort.
CTL decays with τ=42 days. ATL decays with τ=7 days.
