Design notes

The old screen told you what you already knew (distance, time, HR) and then dumped six advanced metrics in a plain list. No route. No context. No reason to come back.

This version reorders the information so the most human parts land first: route, delta-from-normal, coach's interpretation of your analytics. Raw numbers live further down — still one scroll away.

Flow
  1. Hero — distance + delta vs your own baseline
  2. Trio — duration, pace, HR with deltas
  3. Route — gradient-stroked path, elevation, loop
  4. Coach — plain-English read of the analytics
  5. Splits + zones — per-km bars and zone distribution
  6. Trends — sparklines vs last 4 easy runs
  7. Week — session count + streak heat
  8. Partners — who's training today, live + recent
  9. Analytics — the advanced numbers, collapsible
